★ The Through-Line ★

  • Electric structure Every structural border, divider, and separator is electric-purple-tinted. One hue family owns the skeleton.
  • Hot actions Every primary action runs the same hot → electric gradient. If it glows pink, it does something.
  • Glyph motif ★ ◆ ▲ recur in section labels, group labels, and separators. Text characters, never emoji.
